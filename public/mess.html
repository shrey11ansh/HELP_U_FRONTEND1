MESS & CANTEENS

Each hostel has its own mess and according to the students' reviews, the food is not up to the mark but mostly good. To avail of mess facilities, students have to pay an advance which is equal to the mess fee for each semester. At the beginning of every semester, each hosteller needs to pay an amount between INR 19,000 and 20,000 in advance as a mess bill.
