Our Campus

Kamla Nehru Institute of Technology is a government institution located in Sultanpur in Uttar Pradesh. The college has crossed an establishment period of more than 40 years. The college offers various courses at undergraduate, post graduate, and doctorate levels in the stream of engineering and computer applications. Under these streams , the students are provided with more specializations to major in. The admission process takes place online and the B.Tech courses require the students to write the UP state entrance exam. All the admissions take place as per the guidelines of Dr. Abdul Kalam Technological institute. The college provides some key facilities like hostels, dispensary, canteen , guest house etc. The students are also encouraged to participate in various extra-curricular activities apart from academics. There is a special training cell that takes care of the placement needs of the students. The students are not just provided opportunities but also trained for the same. The companies that approach the institution for placements include TCS, Cognizant, Hcl, etc.
